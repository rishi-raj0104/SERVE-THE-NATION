For decades Indian Women And Child Welfare Trust has been working with the most disadvantaged children, women and low income community to provide them better healthcare, education opportunities and protection from physical and mental exploitation. We go right in the middle of the poorest communities with our development projects and help children be children. We rid them from the clutches of malnutrition, send them to schools, help pregnant women deliver safe, pull children out of child labour – all with an aim to give children a happy childhood. It is because of the hundreds and thousands of generous donors that we have been able to pull it so far.
